Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the adoption process?

▼
Browse our "Furry Friends" tab, click "Adopt" to schedule a visit, and once your visit is completed and approved by staff, you'll be able to finalize the adoption directly from your dashboard.

What should I bring to my scheduled visit?

▼
Please bring a valid photo ID and proof of residence. This helps us verify your information and ensures a safe environment for our animals.

Are the pets vaccinated?

▼
Yes! We ensure all pets listed as "Available" have up-to-date vaccinations. You can check the specific vaccination status on each pet's card in the browsing section.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

▼
You can cancel any "Pending" visit directly from your "My Appointments" tab. For rescheduling, please cancel your current request and book a new time slot that works for you.

How long does the approval process take?

▼
Our staff typically reviews visit requests within 24-48 hours. You will see the status update from "Pending" to "Approved" in your appointments log as soon as it's processed.
